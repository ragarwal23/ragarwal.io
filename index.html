Case Studies

Growth Strategy

Growth Strategy for $2B+ Industrials Manufacturer

Large Enterprise · Industrials · Corporate Strategy
Problem
New CSO needed to identify right end-markets and products to unlock growth across three non-adjacent business units in a low-growth industry.
Action
Developed customized framework to shortlist 10+ product categories across 30+ end-markets. Created detailed market map yielding 8+ growth plays. Assessed value chain dynamics and conducted M&A scan identifying 80+ targets.
$500M+ growth opportunities identified. 8+ acquisitions completed. $300M+ top-line impact delivered.
Pricing

Pricing System for $700M Fabless Semiconductor

Large Enterprise · Technology · Commercial Ops
Problem
Needed a system of records for pricing with insights to update price-books and quote-level pricing adjustments.
Action
Built customer and product segmentation, identified pricing discrepancies at quote level, developed Price x Volume curves for top SKUs, and designed process flow for pricing refresh.
$4M impact. 40x ROI.
Commercial Due Diligence

CDD: Trucks & Heavy Equipment Resale Market

Large Enterprise · Industrials · Strategy & Development
Problem
PE firm evaluating PortCo tuck-in acquisition of a logistics broker in the truck and construction equipment resale segment.
Action
Mapped competitive landscape and value chain, evaluated TargetCo positioning, built bottom-up TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, and quantified growth opportunities and synergies.
Delivered independent market study. PE green-lighted the acquisition.
Demand Forecasting

Demand Forecasting for $4B Semiconductor Co.

Large Enterprise · Technology · Operations
Problem
Post-COVID supply shocks left the company unable to forecast demand or optimally allocate supply across 6,000+ customer-SKU pairs.
Action
Segmented portfolio at customer x product level, baselined forecasting, built algorithms for underperforming segments, and created revenue optimization model.
$150M unlock. +10% forecast accuracy. $25M inventory reduction.
